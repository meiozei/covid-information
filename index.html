WHAT'S COVID-19 ALL ABOUT?

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus,
and most people infected by this virus will experience mild to serious respiratory illness,
and most of the time recover without special treatment.
Older people and those who have underlying medical problems are more likely to develop a serious illness.



Is there a prevention to COVID-19?

Well, to prevent COVID-19, here's what you need to do (to slow it down, of course.):

  • 1. Wash your hands with soap and water for 20 seconds! (You might wanna put a song in there just in case!)
  • 2. Maintain a meter distance between you and the person coughing
  • 3. Avoid touching your face. The germs will stick to your face.
  • 4. Cover your mouth and nose when sneezing.
  • 5. Stay at home especially when you feel unwell.
  • 6. Refrain from smoking. (this is a virus that attacks the lungs)
  • 7. Practice social distancing. No, don't satisfy your cravings. Stay at home.

What are the symptoms you may ask?

  • 1. Fever
  • 2. Tiredness
  • 3. Dry Cough
  • 4. Shortness of breath
  • 5. Aches and Pains
  • 6.Sore Throat

Go self-isolate yourselves especially when these symptoms appear in the last 14 days you went out.
